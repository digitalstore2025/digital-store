📖 قصة: يوم في لندن

It's a beautiful day in London! The sun is shining and the sky is blue.

Ahmed and Layla are visiting the famous Big Ben clock. "Look! It's so big!" says Ahmed.

They walk to the Tower Bridge. Many cars and buses cross the bridge.

In the afternoon, they ride the London Eye. "Wow! We can see the whole city!" says Layla. What a wonderful trip!
